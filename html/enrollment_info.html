Enrollment Info

The ICT program has a limited number of spots available each year. To ensure the best candidates are selected, the program includes both admission requirements and an intake procedure. You can take this procedure before you have your school's exam results.

As part of the procedure, you will take a brief intake exam. The results will be communicated to you via email or phone. If you pass the exam, you will be invited to an intake interview. During the interview, we will assess your qualifications, motivation, and suitability for the program.

After the interview, your overall performance will be reviewed to determine if you will be admitted. Please note that failure to attend the scheduled interview will result in the forfeiture of your eligibility for enrollment.

Duration

4 years (full-time program)

Admission Requirements

  • LTS-C diploma, or
  • MULO-B diploma and passing the NATIN entrance exam, or
  • MULO-B diploma with an LTO-C certificate (if the NATIN entrance exam was not passed)


Enrollment

The following documents must be submitted at the time of enrollment:

  • Original and copies of relevant diplomas, grade transcripts, and proof of graduation
  • 2 recent passport-sized photos
  • A recent extract from the civil registry at the CBB (not older than one month)
  • Registration fee and any additional contributions for specific school activities
